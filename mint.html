How to mint your NFT
  1. Create a wallet on Metamask. You can also use Coinbase Wallet, or any other wallet that supports NFTs. Do not use an exchange, such as Coinbase. We recommend using Metamask.
  2. Make sure there is ETH in your wallet. You need slightly more ETH than the mint price to account for gas.
  3. Click the mint button above, and select the quantity you want to mint. You can mint up to 3 NFTs during whitelist sale, at 0.2 ETH each. Public sale is unlimited.
  4. Metamask will pop-up and ask if you want to confirm the transaction. Click confirm.
  5. Your Mythia NFTs will be added to your wallet within a few minutes.
